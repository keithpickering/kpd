Saving earth one pixel at a time

We all have things we're good at - I like to think I'm good at making the world a little nicer to look at.

In a broad sense, I help people turn their ideas into tangible experiences both physical and digital. I work with individuals and businesses, applying my knowledge of design and code to real-world projects, including but not limited to websites, apps, packaging designs, and the like. I believe that the simplest method is the best method when it comes to conveying a message, and I find that idea applies to my designs as well as my code.
